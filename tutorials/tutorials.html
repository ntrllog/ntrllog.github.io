Intro to Coding:

With the exception of HTML/CSS, my goal with the coding "challenges" is to introduce basic concepts of programming. Not the language! That's why they're titled "Intro to CS" and not "Intro to Java".

These are not complete tutorials and should not be treated as such. The hardest part about learning is the beginning and I hope to overcome that difficulty with these.

P.S. With the exception of HTML/CSS, the exercises are pretty much all the same.
